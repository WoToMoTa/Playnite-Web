Description

Trader Life Simulator 2 is a game where you can open your own Supermarket starting from your empty shop and go bigger . Start by selling only grocery products and upgrade your shop to start selling vegetables , fruits , meat , baked products , fashion , toys , houseware , electronics and gaming products .
Customize your shop with +130 equipment / furniture, purchase and manage your vehicles, transport goods and upgrade your business.
